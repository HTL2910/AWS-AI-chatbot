## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Where do I start?**  
A: Read [`AUTONOMOUS_DELIVERY_SUMMARY.md`](./AUTONOMOUS_DELIVERY_SUMMARY.md) first (15 min). Then decide your next step based on your role.

**Q: How long will this take to implement?**  
A: ~14-18 weeks total (6 phases). v0.9.0 (testing) takes 2-3 weeks.

**Q: Can I start with just v0.9.0?**  
A: Yes! v0.9.0 is independent and adds value immediately. See [`PHASE_2_QUICK_START.md`](./PHASE_2_QUICK_START.md).

**Q: What if I only have 1 week?**  
A: Focus on v0.9.0 Phase 2.1 (TestGenerator). See [`PHASE_2_QUICK_START.md`](./PHASE_2_QUICK_START.md) for 5-day plan.

**Q: What are the risks?**  
A: See "Risk Mitigation" in [`AUTONOMOUS_DELIVERY_ROADMAP.md`](./AUTONOMOUS_DELIVERY_ROADMAP.md) and [`AUTONOMOUS_DELIVERY_ARCHITECTURE.md`](./AUTONOMOUS_DELIVERY_ARCHITECTURE.md).

**Q: How do I track progress?**  
A: Use [`IMPLEMENTATION_TRACKER.md`](./IMPLEMENTATION_TRACKER.md) - it has detailed checklists for each phase.

**Q: What if I have questions?**
